mixtape creates a virtual cassette loaded with your top Spotify songs.

We do not store your data. For more information, check out our Privacy Policy or contact us.

Privacy Policy

mixtape uses the Spotify Web API to gather data from your Spotify profile. By choosing to use mixtape, you agree to the use of your Spotify account username and top tracks data.

None of the account data used by mixtape is stored or collected anywhere, and it is never shared with any third parties. All information is used solely for displaying your mixtape and is deleted when your browser session ends.

If you would like to revoke mixtape's permissions to access your Spotify data, you can visit your apps page and click "REMOVE ACCESS", or follow this guide.
