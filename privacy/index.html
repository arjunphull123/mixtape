welcome to mixedify

mixedify creates a virtual mixtape loaded with your top Spotify songs.

You can choose a time range, color, and label for your mixtape.

You can also create your own mixtape from scratch (even without a Spotify account)!

You can click "Burn and share" to generate a unique link to your mixtape.

We do not store your data. For more information, check out our Privacy Policy or contact us.

Privacy Policy

mixedify uses the Spotify Web API to gather data from your Spotify profile. By choosing to use mixedify, you agree to the use of your Spotify account username and top tracks data.

None of the Spotify account data used by mixedify is stored or collected anywhere, and it is never shared with any third parties. All information is used solely for displaying your mixtape and is deleted when your browser session ends. If you choose to burn your mixtape, we only store the titles and URLs of each track, as well as the date and title of your mixtape.

If you would like to revoke mixedify's permissions to access your Spotify data, you can visit your apps page and click "REMOVE ACCESS", or follow this guide.
